Frequently Asked Questions

Who can Donate blood?

Any donor, who is healthy, fit and not suffering from any transmittable diseases can donate blood. Donor must be 18 -60 years age and having a minimum weight of 50Kg can donate blood . A donor can again donate blood after 3 months of your last donation of blood.

When is complain against a user registered?

A hospital can register any complain against a user , if in a user's blood sample they found any thing that is not good for the blood reciever or any health problems in his sample.

How long does a request verification takes?

It depend on the hospital to respond to the blood sample . Rest be assured and you may call the hospital .
